about me

I’m a passionate graphic and web designer with a strong eye for detail and a love for clean, purposeful design. I enjoy creating visually engaging and user-friendly digital experiences that bring ideas to life — from branding and web interfaces to creative visuals. Alongside design, I’m also learning the MERN stack, exploring how to merge creativity with development to build websites that not only look good but also work beautifully. I’m always curious about new tools and trends, and I love collaborating on projects that solve real problems and create meaningful value.
